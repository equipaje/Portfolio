CAMS Suicide Prevention
CAMS is a suicide prevention therapy where clinicians and patients work together to understand suicidality and come up with a treatment plan. Today, CAMS is completed via a series of paper forms. I have been working with the creator of CAMS and psychiatrists in the Seattle area to bring CAMS to the digital world using digital pens and Microsoft Word so that it can reach more people than ever before.

Ink Editor
I worked with a team of engineers and designers to improve the Ink Editor feature in Microsoft Word. Ink Editor enables users to edit documents in a more natural way through the use of proofreading gestures. We added new gestures and improved the way the feature works by leveraging insights from user research.

Ink in Windows Mail
I worked as the primary project manager and designer on a multidisciplinary team to bring digital ink to the Windows Mail app. This set of features enables Mail users to create drawings and annotate images in their emails. The project involved design and engineering coordination across five teams within Microsoft.
