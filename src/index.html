Selected work

Uno Digital

As the founding designer at Uno Health, I led product strategy, design, and branding of Uno Digital, a service that helps Medicare members check their eligibility and enroll in state and federal programs like Medicaid. I drove iterative pilots and experimentation to create software that is accessible to older adults, proving out Uno's digital strategy, and helping thousands of Medicare Members get access to important financial assistance.

Color Behavioral Health

Lead designer for product strategy, vision, and scoping for Color's entry into Behavioral Health. This was a 0-1 business line, aiming to address high need for accessible, low cost, culturally competent behavioral healthcare in the public sector.

Qualtrics Accessibility

I was the accessibility expert on a team of more than 40 designers and researchers. I developed a design pattern library to help teams implement consistent, accessible experiences across the product. I also developed learning resources to educate the team about accessibility and inclusive design. Read an article I wrote on Medium

XM Catalog

I designed end to end features that form the basis of the Qualtrics UX platform, including delivering a northstar framework for the XM Catalog, which enables people to discover and create new projects and workflows in Qualtrics. The XM Catalog currently has 140,000 monthly active users. See a Catalog case study in Figma

Automatic Alt Text

Re-imagining the alt text experience in Microsoft Office. Today, Automatic Alt Text generates alt text for more than 2 million images every day, improving access of documents for everyone. Learn about Automatic Alt Text

EQ Assistant

Leveraging AI and Machine Learning to improve text communication for people with emotional disabilities. Learn about EQ Assistant

Assist

Designing a micro-volunteering platform that enables anyone to request help from volunteers in their community. This case study shows how inclusive design methods result in products that work better for all humans. Learn about Assist

CAMS Suicide Prevention

At Microsoft, I led a partnership with leading psychiatrists to bring the power of digital pen and ink to therapy sessions to help clinicians reach more people experiencing suicidality.

ImgBot

Co-founder and designer for ImgBot, a GitHub app that helps the internet run faster by optimizing images. I designed the logo, pull requests, and the core app website and documentation. ImgBot is installed in more than 300,000 GitHub repositories. See website
